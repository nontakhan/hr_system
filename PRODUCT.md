# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Employees, managers, HR staff, and administrators use the system in day-to-day personnel workflows. They need to submit requests, review approvals, maintain employee records, and inspect operational HR reports accurately and efficiently in a Thai-language work environment.

## Product Purpose

NR Backoffice centralizes HR records and workflows, including attendance, leave, overtime, training, approvals, employee warnings, and reporting. Success means users can complete routine tasks quickly, understand the current state of each workflow, and trust that permissions, scope, dates, and personal information are handled correctly.

## Brand Personality

Formal, easy to use, and trustworthy. The interface should feel calm and familiar to office users, with concise Thai copy and predictable controls.

## Anti-references

Avoid flashy, decorative, or experimental interfaces that distract from operational work. Do not replace familiar Bootstrap patterns with unusual controls, excessive motion, heavy visual effects, or styling inconsistent with the existing system.

## Design Principles

- Make the user's current task and available action immediately clear.
- Prefer familiar, consistent controls over novelty.
- Preserve authorization, privacy, and HR scope in every workflow and report.
- Show useful validation, loading, empty, and error states in clear Thai language.
- Keep dense operational information readable on desktop and usable on smaller screens.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG 2.1 AA. Support keyboard operation, visible focus states, adequate color contrast, understandable labels, semantic controls, readable Thai text, and reduced-motion preferences where motion is present.
